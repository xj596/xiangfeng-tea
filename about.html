About Xiangfeng Tea Group

From plantation to packaging, every step is carefully controlled

Home / About Us

Founded in 1999, Xiangfeng Tea Group has grown from a small family plantation in Hunan Province into one of China's largest B2B tea exporters. With 53,000 mu of organic tea plantations across 6 provinces, 3 state-of-the-art processing facilities, and partnerships with 2,000+ family farms, we deliver premium Chinese teas to over 50 countries worldwide. Our annual production capacity exceeds 50,000 tons.

Production & Processing

From plantation to packaging, every step is carefully controlled

Harvesting

53,000 mu of organic tea plantation across 6 provinces. Hand-picked by 20,000+ skilled workers during peak season, ensuring only the finest buds and leaves are selected.

Sorting & Grading

Rigorous multi-stage selection process using both AI-powered optical sorters and experienced tea masters. Graded by leaf size, shape, color, and aroma profile.

Processing & Fermentation

State-of-the-art facilities with automated temperature and humidity control. Traditional craftsmanship combined with modern processing technology across 32 dedicated production lines.

Packaging & QC

Multi-layer quality inspection at every stage. Metal detection, microbiological testing, and sensory evaluation before final packaging in food-grade, export-compliant materials.

Global Shipping

Efficient logistics network reaching 50+ countries via sea freight, air cargo, and China-Europe Railway Express. Full customs documentation and certificate of origin provided.
