Our Story

Xiangfeng Tea Group was founded in 1999 in Changsha, Hunan Province — one of China's most renowned tea-producing regions. What began as a family tea garden has grown into one of the largest integrated tea enterprises in China.

Today, we manage over 53,000 mu of organic tea plantations, operate state-of-the-art processing facilities, and export premium Chinese tea to more than 50 countries worldwide.

Our commitment to sustainable farming, traditional craftsmanship, and modern quality control has earned us international certifications including EU Organic, USDA Organic, and Rainforest Alliance.
